Weave-A-Tale

LOVE A STORY WITH A TWIST ?

Story telling is a beautiful form of expression. The best stories are an amalgam of genres told not just told by one, but the one told by all. Meraki presents to you an event where your imaginations are the limit,the storyteller presents an outline of a story and the contestants build on it picking up from where the last person finished. A mystery? comedy? romance? One can never tell!

Guidelines

  • Duration - 90 seconds per speaker
  • No inappropriate scenes and language.
  • Avoid sensitive socio-cultural and political topics
  • Negative marking for exceeding time limit.
  • Ensure that mics and cameras are working.
  • Heckling shall result in disqualification.
  • Registrations will take place on a first-come-first-serve basis.
  • Individual event
  • Grade -9-10th grade
  • Each speaker continues to build the story from the last sentence of the contestant prior to them

Judgement

  • Originality
  • Creativity
  • Grammar/ vocabulary
  • Plot structure
  • Character development
  • Expressiveness/voice modulation
  • Fluency
