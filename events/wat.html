Weave-A-Tale

LOVE A STORY WITH A TWIST ?

Story telling is a beautiful form of expression. The best stories are an amalgam of genres not just told by one, but the one told by all. Meraki presents to you an event where your imaginations are the limit,the storyteller presents an outline of a story and the contestants build on it picking up from where the last person finished. A mystery? comedy? romance? One can never tell!

Guidelines

  • Grade -9-10th grade
  • Individual event
  • Duration - 90 seconds per speaker
  • Registrations will take place on a first-come-first-serve basis
  • Each speaker continues to build the story from the last sentence of the contestant prior to them
  • No inappropriate scenes and language
  • Avoid sensitive socio-cultural and political topics
  • There will be negative marking for exceeding time limit
  • Ensure that mics and cameras are working
  • Heckling shall result in disqualification

Judgement

  • Originality
  • Creativity
  • Grammar/ vocabulary
  • Plot structure
  • Character development
  • Expressiveness/voice modulation
  • Fluency
