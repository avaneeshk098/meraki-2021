Funny Bone

Stand up comedy

In this lockdown, us class clowns don’t have much to do, this is the perfect platform for you to showcase your sense of humour. An open mic for all the funny ones out there!

Guidelines

  • Registrations will take place on a first-come-first-serve basis
  • It is an individual event.
  • Grade - 11-12th grade
  • No inappropriate jokes and language. Avoid sensitive socio-cultural and political topics
  • Ensure that mics and cameras are working
  • The event will consist of 2 rounds. In the preliminary round each participant gets 3 minutes and 5 minutes in the final round (more information in the next slide).
  • Negative marking for exceeding time limit.
  • The first round will be topic based, hence everyone is recommended to stick to the topic.
  • The judges scores will be final and binding .
  • No participant can point out any individual or religion in any way, and any dual meaning obscene content is strictly not allowed.

Rubrics

  • Fluency
  • Content
  • Creativity
  • Originality
  • Body language
  • Confidence
