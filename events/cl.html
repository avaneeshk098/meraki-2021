Cluminati

“Let the game be ventured!”

Four friends embark on a thrilling adventure to find their missing friend Mark, who disappears after a college party with his friends. Worried, they go in search for him and come across a bunch of clues he left behind...

Guidelines

  • Registrations will take place on a first-come-first-serve basis
  • Group event - 4 members (max 20 groups)
  • Grade - 11-12th grade
  • Duration - 1 hour 15 minutes
  • Teams will be given clues to decipher as they go about the event.
  • The Cluminati is a time based event, the team that successfully solves all the clues first wins!
  • This event will be held on discord, a server will be made for this event and the participants will be asked to join.
  • Once the event is in motion participants will receive instructions on how to obtain the clues.
  • The participants are to log on/ make a discord account and make sure they can see and talk in the appropriate channels.

Rubrics

Speed

This is a time based event so speed is the most important criteria.

Accuracy

Incase the participants are not able to acquire the correct end answer they will be judged on how accurate or close to the real answer they got.
