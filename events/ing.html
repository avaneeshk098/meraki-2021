Inspired Gallery

Bring your imagination to life by portraying your favourite characters the way you like them.

A FAN ART event for you to envision your desired books/anime and present it through art.

Guidelines

  • Grade 6-8
  • Individual Event
  • 10 minutes for submission after the event
  • Digital & Traditional Art both will be accepted
  • Plagiarism is prohibited
  • Brief description of story/character to be included
  • Submission via GOOGLE CLASSROOM

Judgement

  • Originality
  • Creativity
  • Aesthethic
  • Concept
  • Character development
  • Relevance
