The Imitation Game

Have you ever been in love with a character so much so that you started dressing like them and even talking like them.. If so you have landed in the perfect place.

Bring your favourite character to life by impersonating them with dialogues that moved you! You can also share your thoughts on why this character was so relatable.

Guidelines

  • Grade: 6th-8th
  • Individual Event
  • Each participant gets 3 minutes. Kindly do not exceed the provided time limit.
  • No inappropriate costumes.
  • Props can be included.
  • No inappropriate language/behavior.
  • Make sure the audio and video is working and clear.
  • The character chosen must be mentioned in the google form and cannot be changed later on.

Judgement

Presentation

Confidence, creativity, fluency, vocabulary, facial expressions and body language.

Content

Can include dialogue, give your point of view of the chosen character, imitation of the character (mimicry).
