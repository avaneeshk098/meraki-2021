Evolutus

Have you ever read something and envisioned a different end, maybe even a different middle? Well here's your chance to script a whole new tale just the way YOU want it to be. Meraki 2021 offers you the chance to play God and craft a storyline that's all yours with Evolutus.

You will most definitely not see any limitations to your creativity with this one. Come create your own universe with us :)

Guidelines

  • Grade - 9-10th grade
  • Individual event
  • Submission time : 15 mins
  • Plagiarism is strictly discouraged
  • Do not use abbreviations, slang or any inappropriate words/phrases
  • There is no word limit
  • Entries submitted after the submission time will not be accepted and will be disqualified
  • Prompt will be given on spot
  • Submissions via Google Classrooms

Judgement

Creativity

The use of parallels, keeping in mind the timeline and show, and the use of language (phrases, slangs)

Plot

Title of the story, character development, climax and ending of the story

Vocabulary

Ideas and concepts, creativity, grammar, descriptive language, fresh perspectives, writing style.
