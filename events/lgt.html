Let's Get Trivial!

Books and movies possess the ability to immerse individuals into their stories and experiences. From the magical world of Harry Potter to the mythical stories of Percy Jackson and the MARVELous universe of Avengers have been an experience larger than life, leaving each one of us with a feeling of belonging in these fictious worlds. Meraki brings to you the opportunity to relive these experiences through a trivia contest, Let’s Get Trivial! Compete along with your peers to test your knowledge over some of the most popular books and cinematic series and find out who is the biggest fan indeed!

Guidelines

  • Grade: 6th to 8th
  • The event will be a team based consisting of 5 members each
  • Registrations will take place on a first-come-first-serve basis
  • 4 Rounds: 1 for Harry Potter, 1 for Percy Jackson and 2 for Avengers
  • For each round ONLY one member from each team answers within the given time limit
  • All the members of each team have to have the knowledge of atleast one criteria from the three genres

Judgement

For each right answer given within the time limit, the respective team will earn one point.

For each wrong answer there is negative marking of half mark.

If time limit is exceeded the question is passed onto the next team.
