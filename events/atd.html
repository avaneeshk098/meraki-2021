Guidelines

  • Duration of event - 1 hour
  • Individual event
  • 2 rounds
  • 1st Round , All participants are divided into 2 group & put into break out rooms further divided into 4 groups , 1 for and 1 against in each breakout rooms (Topic will be given before hand)
  • 2nd Round , participants are narrowed 2 one winner from each breakout room , (Rebuttal round) (Topic for round 2 given on spot)
  • Fit in all points within the given time limit (2 mins/person - round 1)
  • Camera must be turned ON at all times
  • Registration form must be filled (Name , Class and school name to be provided)

Rubrics

  • Organisation of points
  • Rebuttal
  • Vocabulary
  • Clarity
  • Fluency
