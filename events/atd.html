Guidelines

  • Grade : 9-10th
  • Individual event
  • Topic 1- The ownership of private land should be limited to a certain time period in metropolitan cities
  • The event will take place in two rounds
  • The participants are to prepare for both for and against the topic for round 1. They will be assigned for or against by the host
  • 1st Round: All participants will be divided into 2 group & put into break out rooms which will be further divided into 4 groups , 1 for and 1 against in each breakout rooms
  • 2nd Round: Participants are narrowed down from each breakout room for the final showdown. Participants in round 2 will be given 10 minutes to gather their thoughts
  • Camera must be turned ON at all times

Judgement

  • Organisation of points
  • Rebuttal
  • Vocabulary
  • Clarity
  • Fluency
