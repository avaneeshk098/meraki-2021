Old Meets New

Travel Through Time

Love watching shows on Netflix? This is the perfect event for you! Play the roles of your favourite characters from your favourite sitcoms… But with a twist! Reimagine these contemporary shows using the vocabulary and slangs of the early twentieth century and turn them into written plays of the Progressive Era—1900’s to 1920’s.

Guidelines

  • Registrations will take place on a first-come-first-serve basis
  • Group event - 3-4 members (8 groups)
  • Grade - 11-12th grade
  • Duration - 6 minutes per group
  • Participants must choose one sitcom from the given five.
  • Timeline - 1900-1920's [Progressive Era]
  • Use of laugh tracks (optional)*
  • Costumes (optional)*
  • Language, slangs, phrases, themes, style of conversation relevant to the timeline
  • No inappropriate scenes and language. Avoid sensitive socio-cultural and political topics
  • Negative marking for exceeding time limit.
  • Soft copy of script to be submitted the day before(9:00pm)
  • Fair distribution of dialogue (teamwork)
  • Ensure that mics and cameras are working

Rubrics

Creativity - 5 points

The use of parallels, keeping in mind the timeline and show, and the use of language (phrases, slangs)

Delivery - 5 points

Intonation, expression (facial, verbal), ease, timing, fluency, (reaction of the audience would be taken into account)

Content - 5 points

Quality, accuracy with reference to timeline and the sitcom, script (cohesive and coherent)

Guide

The 1900’s and 1920’s have a few important themes such as industrialisation, aftermath of war, materialism, advocacy of rights, etc. These themes could be used to draw parallels between the two timelines.
