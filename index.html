About Meraki 2021

English literature is a glorious inheritance which is open to all. In the English language and literature there are riches and treasures for us to enjoy and unravel. DPS Bangalore South proudly presents MERAKI 2021, Its first virtual inter school literary fest. Meraki is derived from modern Greek often used to describe doing something with soul, creativity, or love. Here you weave a piece of yourself into what you’re doing.

We would be delighted if you would participate in this intellectually stimulating yet fun -filled event. Let us come together and celebrate this joyous festival of literature
